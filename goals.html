My Goals & 90-Day Plan

I am a "hands-on" leader ready to build. My plan is to drive operational excellence and cultivate a "people-first" culture that aligns with the values of Mint and Westbow. Here is my proactive plan for the first 90 days.

My First 90 Days: Driving Operational Excellence

Assuming I earn the role, here is my plan to align teams, optimize production, and foster a culture of continuous improvement.

First 30 Days

Listen, Learn & Align

  • Immerse in Operations: Embed with the leads of Sales, Drafting, Marketing, Production, Procurement, and Quality Control to understand current workflows, challenges, and hand-off points.
  • Build Team Relationships: Meet 1-on-1 with all department heads and key team members. My goal is to listen to their insights, understand their perspectives, and build trust.
  • Understand the Values: Deep-dive with leadership (reporting to the Director of Operations) to align on the core Westbow values—"work smart," "build lasting value," "act with integrity," and "care"—and how they translate to the production floor.
  • Map the Value Stream: Create a high-level map of the entire process from "order received" to "home delivered" to identify initial, obvious bottlenecks or gaps.
First 60 Days

Analyze & Strategize

  • Data-Driven Deep Dive: Analyze all available data on production schedules, quality control reports, material costs, and labor efficiency. Establish baseline KPIs to measure future improvements.
  • Identify "Quick Wins": Target 1-2 key areas for immediate "continuous improvement" that will reduce friction for the team and demonstrate a "work smart" philosophy.
  • Develop Initial Strategic Plan: Draft a 6-month operational plan focused on optimizing resource allocation, improving cross-functional communication, and enhancing quality assurance without sacrificing the "hand-built" standard.
  • Present Findings: Deliver a comprehensive "State of Operations" report to senior leadership, outlining key findings, opportunities, and the proposed 6-month plan.
First 90 Days

Execute & Foster Culture

  • Implement "Quick Wins": Execute the 1-2 process improvements identified in the first 60 days, clearly communicating the "why" to the entire team and measuring the immediate impact.
  • Establish Rhythms: Implement a regular cadence of operational meetings (e.g., weekly production planning, monthly cross-department reviews) to foster accountability, collaboration, and transparency.
  • Empower the Team: Begin formalizing processes for "people development." Identify high-potential team members and create opportunities for them to grow.
  • Refine & Report: Refine the 6-month operational plan based on initial execution and feedback. Present a clear 90-Day Review to leadership, highlighting progress, metrics, and the roadmap ahead.

Long Term Aspirations

Beyond 90 days, my goal is to build a lasting, scalable operation that serves as a pillar of the Westbow Group.

Build a World-Class Operation: My primary goal is to build a scalable, efficient, and data-driven manufacturing operation that is renowned for its quality and "integrity."

Preserve the Craft: Ensure that as we scale, we always protect the "hand-built" craftsmanship and "true integrity" that defines the Mint brand. The goal is to "work smart" to make quality *more* consistent, not sacrifice it for speed.

Be a "People-First" Leader: Uphold the Westbow value to "care" by actively developing the next generation of leaders within Mint. I aim to build a "high-performing team" where people feel valued, empowered, and have space to grow.

Drive Lasting Value: Grow with the Westbow Group, contributing to its long-term vision of "building lasting value" and creating meaningful, sustainable communities through operational excellence.

Immediate Learning & Growth Areas

As a leader, I am always learning. These are areas I'm excited to dive into.

Modular Manufacturing Innovation

Master the specific technologies, materials, and compliance standards (CSA, RVIA) unique to the modular and tiny home industry.

Westbow Group Synergy

Learn the strengths of all sister companies (Westbow Capital, Prokey, etc.) to identify opportunities for internal collaboration and efficiency.
